Geocities

Geocities was a website invented to give people "free realestate" on the internet. It allowed people to easily create webstites of their own and connecst with people with simalare interests as them. Brandon Stanton creates geocities, giving each peorson a space on the internet with 15MB of space to use. Eventually Yahoo bought the website after it grew popular in a multi-billion dollar deal. In 2009, Yahoo shut down the site. Many sites were lost, including family photo albums, personal pages, and writing pages.
