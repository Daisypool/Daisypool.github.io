Stairs to Nowhere Tournament

  1. Enter the number of people playing in the Number Of Participants selector.
    • Please do not reload the page. It will mess with the counters. :)
    • You must set the number of participants or the website will not work as intended.
  2. Everyone starts on the first stair.
  3. Find someone on the same stair as you and play rock paper scissors using the buttons on the website.
  4. If you win, press the won button and walk up a stair. Press the win button each time you win a game. If you lose, stay on the same stair and play again with someone on the same stair as you.
  5. If you are the only one of your stair level, you lose. press the Last One On stair button to see your place and stay on your stair.
  6. Continue playing steps 3-5 Until there is one person on each stair level.
  7. The person at the hightest stair wins! :)
