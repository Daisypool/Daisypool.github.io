Genshin Impact

About

Released on September 28, 2020, Genshin Impact has become a well-known Open-world RPG game with over 66 million players. It has made over 5 billion dollars since release, making it one of the largest earning current games. The game follows a chosen protagonist, Aether or Lumine, as they try to find their lost sibling and learn more about the world around them. Currently there are 89 playable characters in game. Besides the story, the game has well built combat mechanics, with each character wielding one of seven elements. These elements react with each other, increasing damage, dealing debuffs, or otherwise helping the player in combat.

Persona5 Royal

About

Persona 5 Royal is an expanded release of Persona 5 and came out in October 31st, 2019. Persona is an RPG game with around 120 hours worth of content. The story follows a group of Highschoolers who create a vigilante group that targets corrupted adults. They quickly find themselves in for more than they originally planned. The gameplay is heavily story based, but the combat mechanics are turn based. Although the game is long, there are a total of 5 main endings.

Broken Reality

About

Broken reality is an exploration game, where the focus is to just walk around areas solving puzzles and talking to the NPCs. It was released November 29th, 2018. The very bold colors and use of soundtrack gives the game a great atmosphere, which makes the game enjoyable. This is an Indie game and is relatively unknown, but still is a fantastic game that I recommend. The game is set in NATEM, a virtual world based on the history of the internet. The player explores several areas, each with its own distinct theme. While there is not much story, the game is still engaging and makes each little thing you learn more valuable. The developers are currently working on a squeal called Broken Reality 2000 which will come out later this year.
