Genshin Impact

About

Released September 28, 2020, Genshin Impact has become a well known Open-World RPG game with over 66 million players. It has made over 5 billion dollars since release, making it one of the largest earning current games. The game follows a chosen protaganist, Aether or Lumine, as they try to find their lost sibling and learn more about the world around them. Currently there are 89 playable characters in game. Besides from the story, the game has well built combat mechanices, with each character weilding one out of seven elements. These elements react with eachother, increasing damgae, dealing debuffs, or otherwise helping the player in combat.
